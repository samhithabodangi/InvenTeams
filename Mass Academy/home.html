Diversifying Optical Technology (DOT.)

Hello! We are a group of high school seniors attending the Massachusetts Academy of Math and Science at WPI and are currently in the process of applying for the Lemelson-MIT InvenTeams Grant. Over the past year, we have been working towards developing a device called DOT, which aims to address issues faced by the visually impaired and blind community such as braille literacy and the lack of affordable and portable braille displays.
